# Paranatural Topos

## Status: Consolidated

Merged into `parametricity-free-theorems.md`.

The central question ("is there a topos of profunctors with
paranatural morphisms?") is resolved:
`PshParametricPresheaf C` (= `PshRelSpanObj C ⥤ Type`) is
a copresheaf category, hence a Grothendieck topos. Negative
results about `DiagDetProf` and the diagonalization monad are
preserved as context in the unified workstream.
